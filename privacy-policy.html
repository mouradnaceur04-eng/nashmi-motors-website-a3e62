Privacy Policy

Nashmi Motors — 8001 Paxton St, Harrisburg, PA 17111

Last updated: January 2026

1. Who We Are

Nashmi Motors ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent used car dealership located at 8001 Paxton St, Harrisburg, PA 17111. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when using our website at nashmimotors.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our website forms, including:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, mailing address
  • Vehicle preferences: make, model, price range, body type
  • Finance application data: Social Security Number (SSN), date of birth, employment information, income details, housing information, trade-in details, and co-buyer information — collected only when you submit a credit application
  • Test drive requests: name, phone, email, preferred date and time
  • Vehicle sale inquiries: vehicle details and contact information when using our "We Buy Cars" form

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely to:

  • Contact you regarding your inquiry, test drive request, or vehicle purchase
  • Process and evaluate your financing application
  • Submit your credit application to lenders on your behalf (finance leads only)
  • Route your inquiry to our sales team via email

Finance application data (including SSN and DOB) is transmitted securely to our dealership management system (DealerCenter) and to financing lenders for credit evaluation purposes only.

4. How We Protect Your Information

All form submissions are transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. Finance applications are forwarded in encrypted format to our CRM system. We do not store sensitive financial data (SSN, DOB, bank account numbers) on our website servers.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is shared only with:

  • Our internal sales team at Nashmi Motors
  • Financing lenders when you submit a credit application
  • Our dealership management system provider (DealerCenter) for lead processing

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use standard browser cookies to maintain basic site functionality. We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking. If analytics tools are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services:

  • CarFax: Vehicle history reports are linked directly to CarFax's website. CarFax's own privacy policy governs data on their platform.
  • DealerCenter: Our inventory management and CRM platform. Finance leads are transmitted to DealerCenter for processing.
  • Google Maps / Fonts: Used for maps and typography. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion or correction of any personal information you have provided to us by contacting us directly. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
